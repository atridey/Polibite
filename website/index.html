Polibite: Politics in Bite-Sized Pieces.

Our Mission

Proper legislation is a cornerstone for democracy in our country. However, an alarming portion of U.S. citizens rarely keep up with legislative debates and changes. Through Polibite, we allow users to summarize all of the relevant news in Congress on any given day. It only takes a few minutes to read, and we hope it helps you be more informed and confident about legislative changes, one bite at a time. We also encourage you to register for voting if you haven't already and to check out our page to learn more about the legistlative system. Click the buttons below to start!
